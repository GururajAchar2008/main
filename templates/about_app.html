About Customer Service Management App

This application is designed to streamline the management of customer details and service records for businesses dealing with customer service, such as RO water purifier services.

Key Features:

  • User Authentication: Secure login system with distinct roles for Super Admin, Admin, and regular Users/Staff.
  • Role-Based Access Control:
    • Super Admin: Full control, including adding new Admin/User accounts, and all Admin functionalities.
    • Admin: Manage customers (add, view, edit, remove), and add service records.
    • User/Staff: View customers, add new customers, edit existing customer details, and add service records for existing customers.
  • Customer Management:
    • Add new customer details (name, contact, address, unique ID, RO model/color).
    • View a comprehensive list of all customers.
    • Search functionality to quickly find customers by various criteria.
    • Edit existing customer details.
    • Soft-delete customers (marks as removed, but retains historical data).
  • Service Tracking:
    • Record detailed service information for each customer.
    • Select from a predefined list of service parts/items with quantity.
    • Ability to add additional service charges and a description of the problem/solution.
    • Automatic calculation of total service cost.
    • View complete service history for each customer, including who performed the service and when.
  • Dashboard: Provides an overview of total customers and total services performed.

Purpose:

The primary goal of this application is to provide an efficient and organized way to manage customer interactions and service history, improving customer satisfaction and operational efficiency for service-oriented businesses.
